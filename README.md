# Hacker Cup

Meta's annual open programming competition for participants worldwide.

## About

Hacker Cup is an international coding competition that challenges participants to apply their problem-solving and algorithmic coding skills. Competitors advance through a series of online rounds, with opportunities to win prizes and potentially reach the global finals for the grand prize.

## Competition Format

The 2025 Hacker Cup season features two divisions:

**Human Division**: Compete as you normally would, writing your own solutions to programming challenges.

**AI Division**: All solutions must be written by a computer, testing the capabilities of artificial intelligence in competitive programming.

## Important Dates

- Registration opens: October 3rd
- Practice round begins: October 10th

## How It Works

1. Register for the competition when registration opens
2. Participate in the practice round to familiarize yourself with the format
3. Compete in online qualification rounds
4. Advance through successive rounds by solving algorithmic problems
5. Top performers may qualify for the global finals

## Getting Started

Visit the official Hacker Cup website to register and find more information about rules, problem formats, and competition guidelines.

## Who Can Participate

The competition is open to participants from around the world. Whether you are a student, professional developer, or coding enthusiast, you are welcome to test your skills.
